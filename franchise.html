Franchise Requirements

  • Space: Minimum 200-500 Sq. ft. area required for classrooms and lab.
  • Computers: Minimum 5-10 computers with internet connectivity.
  • Faculty: Qualified teaching staff (minimum 2).
  • Facilities: Clean drinking water, electricity backup, and washroom.
