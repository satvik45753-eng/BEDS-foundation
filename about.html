About BESD Foundation

Empowering society through education, skill development, and social welfare.

Who We Are

The Bambdeo Education and Social Development Foundation (BESD) is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to bridging the gap between ambition and opportunity. Established with the vision of creating an educated and self-reliant society, we focus on providing high-quality vocational and computer education to students from all walks of life.

Our foundation believes that education is the most powerful tool for social change. Through our network of study centers and expert faculty, we aim to equip the youth with the skills necessary to succeed in the modern digital economy.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, accessible, and industry-relevant education to empower the youth and contribute to nation-building.

Our Vision

To become a leading organization in the field of skill development and social welfare, creating a knowledgeable and skilled society.

Core Values

Integrity, Inclusivity, Excellence, and Social Responsibility remain at the heart of every initiative we undertake.

Our Key Objectives

Promote computer literacy in rural and urban areas.

Provide vocational training for employment generation.

Organize social awareness camps for health and education.

Empower women and underprivileged sections of society.

Collaborate with government and other NGOs for development projects.

Establish study centers to reach remote locations.
